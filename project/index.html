  • Folktales around the World
    This app, called “Rosebush” is intended to help users reflect on their day by completing an exercise called “rose-bud-thorn” each day. The “rose” is something good that happened that day, the “thorn” is something not so great that happened, and the “bud” is something they’re looking forward to. The app provides a guided way to do this reflection and share it with friends and family.
